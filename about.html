About Me

Hi, I'm Rahul Balaram Gupta, originally from India and now an international student in Canada. My interest in computers sparked during high school, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's in Computer Application (BCA) at Tilak Maharashtra University, India. In my final year of BCA, I took on a project that laid the foundation for my journey into AI - a House Price Prediction model using machine learning. Since then, my passion for technology has only grown. Constantly adapting to the ever-changing tech landscape, I'm a perpetual learner. Currently, I'm enhancing my skills through formal education in cloud computing. The dynamic nature of IT keeps me excited about the possibilities ahead. Join me on this journey as I navigate the intersections of technology and education, driven by curiosity and a commitment to staying ahead in the world of IT.
